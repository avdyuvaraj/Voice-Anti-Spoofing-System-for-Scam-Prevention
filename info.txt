🔬 4️⃣ Feature Extraction (Core Intelligence)

You used MFCC + Delta + Delta²

What is MFCC?

MFCC = Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients

It represents:

Human-perceived frequency spectrum

Shape of the vocal tract

Speech characteristics

It is widely used in:

Speech recognition

Speaker verification

Audio classification

Why Delta Features?

MFCC = static spectral shape
Delta = rate of change
Delta² = acceleration of change

This helps detect:

Robotic smoothness in synthetic speech

Natural micro variations in human speech

🤖 5️⃣ Machine Learning Model

RandomForestClassifier

Why?

Works well on small datasets

Handles non-linear patterns

Resistant to overfitting

No heavy tuning required

It builds multiple decision trees and votes for the final class.

🔍 11️⃣ How Detection Actually Works (Explain Simply)

When audio is uploaded:

Convert to 16kHz

Normalize volume

Extract MFCC + delta features

Feed into trained RandomForest

Get probability scores

Segment into 1-second chunks

Predict each chunk

Compute synthetic percentage

Display timeline

Generate report if needed
